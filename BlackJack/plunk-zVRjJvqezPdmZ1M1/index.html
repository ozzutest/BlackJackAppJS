Welcome to Blackjack!

There is some rules about Blackjack game.
  1. Player has to beat a dealer(croupier), earning fully or nearest 21 points from your two random cards.
  2. Jack, Queen, King have value of 10 points.
  3. Ace has value from 1 to 11.
  4. Cards from values [2, 10] have their own value.
  5. If you would like to increase the value of your cards - click hit button.
  6. If you would like to end game with beneficial score - click stay button.
